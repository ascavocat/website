Why choose ASC Lawyer?




Immigration Lawyer serving clients from Montreal, Laval, Longueuil ...

ASC Lawyer is an immigration lawyer firm dedicated to providing you with appropriate legal advice and solutions to your immigration problems. ASC Immigration Lawyer's goal is to help its clients find appropriate solutions to their immigration problems and to protect their rights both in the immigration process and in court as need.

Our Approach as an immigration lawyer

We provide our clients with sound advice and zealous representation to achieve excellent results in accordance with their immigration plans. With extensive knowledge in immigration matters, ASC Lawyer has the ability to handle your cases in order to make your immigration project a reality and allow you to settle in Canada. We understand the issues at stake for our clients and are dedicated to finding appropriate solutions on their behalf before the appropriate authorities.

Our services in immigration law are in 2 parts:

  1. Immigration procedures for temporary or permanent residence, and humanitarian immigration;

  2. Administrative and judicial proceedings.

With more than 80 immigration programs in Canada, ASC Immigration Lawyer assist our clients in analyzing their immigration project, advising them on the risks, and carrying out on their behalf the administrative or legal procedures related to immigration.

The immigration lawyer firm, ASC Lawyer, acts for its clients in various immigration situations:

  • student visa
  • tourist or business visa
  • work permit
  • Application for permanent residence
  • Refugee claim or asylum
  • Humanitarian application
  • Application for sponsorship
  • Etc.

In addition, ASC Immigration Lawyer intervene when our clients encounter obstacles in their immigration project in order to vigorously defend their rights in various situations:

  • Sponsorship Refusal
  • Loss of permanent residency
  • Loss of refugee status
  • An admissibility hearing before the Immigration Division for removal orders (criminality, misrepresentation, residency obligation, etc.)
  • Appeal of a removal order to the Immigration Appeal Division
  • Refugee Appeal Division process
  • Immigration Appeal Division process
  • Federal Court Application for Review
  • Etc.

If you need an immigration lawyer for any immigration issues, please contact us today to schedule a consultation. We represent clients before Quebec Immigration and Canada immigration.
