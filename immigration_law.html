Why choose ASC Lawyer?




Immigration Law Firm

ASC Avocat is an immigration law firm dedicated to providing you with appropriate legal advice and solutions to your immigration problems. ASC Avocat's goal is to help its clients find appropriate solutions to their immigration problems and to protect their rights both in the immigration process and in court.

Our Approach

We provide our clients with sound advice and zealous representation to achieve excellent results in accordance with their immigration plans. With extensive knowledge in immigration matters, ASC Avocat has the ability to handle your cases in order to make your immigration project a reality and allow you to settle in Canada. We understand the issues at stake for our clients and are dedicated to finding appropriate solutions on their behalf before the appropriate authorities.

Our services in immigration law are in 2 parts:

  1. Immigration procedures for temporary or permanent residence, and humanitarian immigration;

  2. Administrative and judicial proceedings.

With more than 80 immigration programs in Canada, we assist our clients in analyzing their immigration project, advising them on the risks, and carrying out on their behalf the administrative or legal procedures related to immigration.

We act for our clients in various immigration situations:

  • Application for a student visa
  • Application for a tourist or business visa
  • Application for a work permit
  • Application for permanent residence
  • Application for asylum
  • Humanitarian application
  • Application for sponsorship
  • Etc.

In addition, we intervene when our clients encounter obstacles or problems in their immigration project in order to vigorously defend their rights in various situations:

  • Sponsorship Refusal
  • Loss of permanent residency
  • Loss of refugee status
  • An admissibility hearing before the Immigration Division for removal orders (criminality, misrepresentation, residency obligation, etc.)
  • Appeal of a removal order to the Immigration Appeal Division
  • Appeal to the Refugee Appeal Division
  • Appeal to the Immigration Appeal Division
  • Federal Court Application for Review
  • Etc.
